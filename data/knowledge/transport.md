# Ground Transport — Meridian Regional Airport

## Taxi
The taxi stand is outside the Arrival Hall on Level 1 (follow the orange "Taxi" signs). Metered fare to the city centre is approximately $25–35 and takes around 30 minutes. Queues are longest 18:00–20:00. An airport surcharge of $5 applies.

## Ride-hailing (Grab and others)
Ride-hailing pick-up is at Door 4, Level 1, clearly marked "Ride-Hailing Pick-up". Book only after collecting your baggage — drivers wait a maximum of 5 minutes.

## Airport shuttle bus
The city shuttle departs every 30 minutes from Bay 2, Level 1, between 06:00 and 23:00. Fare is $10 per adult and $5 per child, payable by card or cash at the kiosk beside Bay 2. It stops at all major downtown hotels.

## Public bus
Public buses 88 and 121 serve the airport from Bay 1, Level 1. Bus 88 runs to the central railway station (fare $2.50, about 50 minutes). Card payment only.

## Train
There is no direct rail link to the airport. The nearest station is Central Railway Station, reachable by public bus 88 or taxi.

## Car rental
Car rental counters (Avis, Hertz, and a local operator) are in the Arrival Hall, Level 1, open 07:00–22:00. Rental cars are collected from the car park across the arrival driveway.

## Parking
The multi-storey car park is connected to the terminal by a covered link bridge on Level 2. Rates: $2 per 30 minutes, capped at $40 per day. Payment by card at the exit gantry.
