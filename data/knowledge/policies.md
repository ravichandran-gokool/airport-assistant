# Travel Rules and Policies — Meridian Regional Airport

## Liquids in hand luggage
Liquids, aerosols, and gels in carry-on baggage must be in containers of 100 ml or less, all fitting inside one transparent resealable bag of up to 1 litre per passenger. Larger volumes must go in checked baggage. Baby food and prescribed medication are exempt but must be declared at security.

## Prohibited items
Sharp objects, tools over 6 cm, flammable items, and lithium batteries above 160 Wh are not allowed in carry-on baggage. Power banks must be carried in hand luggage (not checked) and must not exceed 100 Wh without airline approval.

## Check-in and boarding cut-off times
For regional flights, check-in counters close 45 minutes before departure and boarding gates close 20 minutes before departure. Passengers should arrive at the airport at least 2 hours before departure.

## Immigration and transit
All departing passengers clear immigration after security on Level 2. Automated e-gates are available for passengers holding biometric passports from participating countries. Transit passengers remaining airside do not clear immigration.

## Baggage allowance
Baggage allowances are set by each airline, not the airport. Typical regional economy allowance is 20–30 kg checked and 7 kg cabin. Check your booking confirmation or ask this assistant to connect you to a human agent for airline contacts.

## Pets and animals
Pets travel as checked or manifest cargo depending on the airline; arrangements must be made with the airline in advance. Guide dogs accompanying passengers are permitted in the terminal.
