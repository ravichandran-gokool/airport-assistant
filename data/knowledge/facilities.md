# Facilities — Meridian Regional Airport

## Toilets
Toilets are located on every level: beside Check-in Row 3 (Level 2), next to Gates A4 and B2 (Level 2, after security), and beside the Arrival Hall exits (Level 1). Accessible toilets are available at all locations.

## Wi-Fi
Free Wi-Fi is available throughout the terminal. Connect to the network "MRA-FreeWifi" — no password or registration required. Charging points with USB and power sockets are located at every gate seating area and in the Level 3 food court.

## ATMs and money changers
ATMs are located in the Arrival Hall (Level 1) and beside Check-in Row 1 (Level 2). A licensed money changer operates in the Arrival Hall daily from 07:00 to 22:00.

## Nursing room and family facilities
A nursing room with a diaper-changing station is located on Level 2 beside the toilets at Check-in Row 3, and another after security next to Gate B2. A children's play corner is in the Level 3 food court.

## Prayer room
A multi-faith prayer room is located on Level 3, next to the food court, open 24 hours.

## Smoking area
Smoking is only permitted in the designated smoking room on Level 3 (outdoor terrace beside the food court) and outside the terminal at the Level 1 taxi stand. Smoking anywhere else in the terminal is prohibited.

## Lost and found
The Lost and Found counter is at the Information Counter in the Arrival Hall (Level 1), open 06:00–23:00. Items found airside are held by airport security for 30 days. Call +65 6100 0000 or ask this assistant to connect you to a human agent.

## Luggage storage
Baggage storage is available beside the Arrival Hall Information Counter, charged at $8 per bag per day. Open 06:00–23:00.

## Pharmacy and medical
A pharmacy is located on Level 2 near Check-in Row 5, open 08:00–21:00. For medical emergencies, a 24-hour first-aid clinic is on Level 1 next to the Arrival Hall — or alert any staff member.
