# Terminal Layout and Wayfinding — Meridian Regional Airport

## Terminal layout overview
The airport has a single terminal with three levels. Level 1 is the Arrival Hall: baggage claim, taxi stand, buses, car rental, and the arrival information counter. Level 2 is the Departure Hall: check-in Rows 1–5, security screening, and Gates A1–A6 and B1–B5 (after security). Level 3 has the food court, lounge, prayer room, and smoking terrace. Lifts and escalators connect all levels at both ends of the terminal.

## Getting to the gates
From the check-in rows on Level 2, walk towards the centre of the hall to the security screening checkpoint. After security, A gates (A1–A6) are to the left and B gates (B1–B5) are to the right. Walking time from security to the furthest gate (B5) is about 8 minutes.

## From arrivals to ground transport
After collecting baggage in the Arrival Hall (Level 1), exit through the green customs channel. The taxi stand is straight ahead through the main doors; ride-hailing pick-up is at Door 4 to the right; bus bays 1 and 2 are to the left.

## Finding check-in rows
Enter the Departure Hall on Level 2. Row 1 is nearest the western entrance (Door 1), Row 5 nearest the eastern entrance (Door 6). Overhead screens above each row show the airlines currently checking in.

## Transfers and connections
Transfer passengers who do not need to collect baggage follow the purple "Transfers" signs from the arrival gate to the transfer security checkpoint on Level 2, then proceed to their departure gate. Minimum recommended connection time is 60 minutes.

## Accessibility routes
Step-free routes connect all levels via lifts at the east and west ends of the terminal. Accessible parking is on Level 2 of the car park, next to the link bridge.
