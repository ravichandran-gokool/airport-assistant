# Passenger Services — Meridian Regional Airport

## Check-in
Check-in counters are on Level 2, arranged in Rows 1–5. Counters open 3 hours before departure and close 45 minutes before departure for regional flights. Self-service check-in kiosks are located at the entrance of Rows 2 and 4.

## Special assistance and wheelchairs
Passengers needing wheelchair or mobility assistance should inform their airline at booking, or approach the Special Assistance counter beside Check-in Row 1. Assistance is free of charge. A staff member will accompany the passenger through security to the gate.

## Baggage services
Damaged or missing baggage should be reported at your airline's baggage services counter in the Arrival Hall before leaving the baggage claim area. Baggage wrapping is available beside Check-in Row 5 for $12 per bag.

## Information counters
Staffed information counters are located in the Arrival Hall (Level 1) and the Departure Hall (Level 2), open 06:00–23:00. Outside these hours, this AI assistant and the duty manager (via any courtesy phone) are available.

## Unaccompanied minors
Unaccompanied minor services are arranged directly with the airline at least 48 hours before the flight. Meeting point is the airline's check-in row.

## Tax (GST) refund
Tourists may claim GST refunds at the eTRS self-service kiosk located after security, next to Gate A1. Claims must be made before checking in goods; for bulky goods, visit the Customs inspection counter beside Check-in Row 1 first.

## Lounge
The Meridian Lounge is after security on Level 3, open 05:00–23:00. Entry via Priority Pass, selected business-class tickets, or walk-in at $45 per person for 3 hours, including food and showers.
